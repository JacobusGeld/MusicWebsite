Welcome!

My name is Jacobus and this is my website that I dedicated to my love for music! Here you will find a list of my favourite songs, artists and instruments!

My love for music started as a kid. My mom taught me how to play guitar and my dad taught me how to play piano. I was hooked almost instantly! They gave me the foundation that I needed to carry on learning and playing these instruments and that's exactly what I did. Just about every big event in my life has a song associated with it. I use music to relax, to have fun, to think and to enjoy life! I don't know what my life would be like without music but I certainly know it's pretty amazing with it.
